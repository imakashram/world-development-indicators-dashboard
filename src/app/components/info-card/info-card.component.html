World Development Indicators GDP & Populations
 
 

GDP (current US$)

Gross Domestic Product (GDP) measures the total value of all goods and services produced within a country's borders in a given year, expressed in current U.S. dollars. It reflects the overall economic activity and size of the economy without adjusting for inflation.
Source: World Bank, OECD, national statistical organizations.

Population

This indicator represents the total number of people residing in a country, regardless of citizenship or legal status, based on midyear estimates. It follows the de facto definition of population.
Source: United Nations, national statistical offices, Eurostat.
